	Sniper System		by R. Von Quest
------------------------------------------------------

A Sniper is a highly trained marksman who operates alone, 
in a pair, or with a sniper team to maintain close visual 
contact with the enemy and engage targets from concealed 
positions or distances exceeding the detection capabilities 
of enemy personnel.

In addition to marksmanship, military snipers are trained 
in camouflage, field craft, infiltration, special 
reconnaissance and observation, surveillance and target 
acquisition.
