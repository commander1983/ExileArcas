    SOCOM MISSION GENERATOR	by R. Von Quest
------------------------------------------------------

Special Operations Command. SOCOM conducts several covert 
and clandestine missions, such as direct action, special
reconnaissance, counter-terrorism, foreign internal 
defense, unconventional warfare, psychological warfare,
civil affairs, and counter-narcotics operations. 

Each branch has a Special Operations Command that is 
unique and capable of running its own operations, but 
when the different special operations forces need to
work together for an operation, SOCOM becomes the joint 
component command of the operation, instead of a SOC of 
a specific branch.
